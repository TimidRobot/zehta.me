Air Cleaning and Cat Safe Plants

2020 Sep 24, Timid Robot Zehta

I'm moving to a new apartment soon and it has a good number of windows with lots of light. I'm excited to buy some plants and I wanted to know which ones are non-toxic to Mochi cat, have been shown to help clean the air, and are easier to acquire and care for.

Recommended

I like the following plants:

  • Bamboo palm (Chamaedorea seifrizii)
    • non-toxic to cats per ASPCA
  • Boston fern (Nephrolepis exaltata 'Bostoniensis')
    • non-toxic to cats per ASPCA
    • Royal Horticultural Society (RHS): Award of Garden Merit (AGM)
  • Lady palm (Rhapis excelsa)
    • non-toxic to cats per ASPCA
    • Royal Horticultural Society (RHS): Award of Garden Merit (AGM)
  • Parlour palm (Chamaedorea elegans)
    • non-toxic to cats per ASPCA
    • Royal Horticultural Society (RHS): Award of Garden Merit (AGM)
  • Pygmy date palm (Phoenix roebelenii)
    • non-toxic to cats per ASPCA
    • Royal Horticultural Society (RHS): Award of Garden Merit (AGM)
